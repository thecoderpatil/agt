`dynamic_exit_threshold(redeploy_yield, wait_months, cc_yield, recovery_prob)` in `agt_deck/risk.py`.

Formula: `d* = (a^W - b^W) / (a^W - b^W + q)` where:
- `a = 1 + Y/12` (monthly redeploy return)
- `b = 1 + y_cc/12` (monthly CC yield on frozen position)
- `q` = recovery probability

Three regimes:
1. d* < 5%: freeze + CC harvest (position near basis)
2. 5% < d* < 25%: exit decision depends on redeploy opportunity
3. d* > 25%: strong exit signal
